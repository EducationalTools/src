How to Play

RULES
  1. Each player will draw one tile from the bag. The tile closest to the letter "A" (or blank) will allow the player to go first. Replace tiles after this inital drawing.
  2. Each player starts their turn by drawing seven tiles from the bag. There are three options in every turn. Players must choose ONE of the following:
    • Place a word.
    • Exchange tiles. Can exchange one or all of the tiles on the rack.
    • Pass to the next player. If any player passes two times in a row the game ends.
  3. When the game begins, the first player will place their word on the star spin in the center of the board. The star is a double square and will offer a double word score. Play continues in a clockwise manner.
  4. Once tiles are placed on the board the player will receive new tiles.
  5. If the player uses 7 tiles to create a word on the board they will receive a 50 point bonus.
  6. Once the bag is emptied and a single player has placed all of their tiles, the game will end and the player with the highest score wins.
  7. Scores are counted at the end of the game. The player who ended the game with no tiles will receive the tile values of the other players leftovers as a bonus.
